Small Commercial Building Energy Load Analysis and Solar Feasibility Study

This project analyses a simulated 30-day electricity demand profile for a small commercial building and evaluates the impact of a 30 kW rooftop solar PV scenario.

Project Overview

An Excel-based dashboard was developed to calculate total energy use, peak demand, estimated electricity cost, emissions, overnight base load, solar self-consumption, exported solar energy, grid import after solar and simple payback.

Key Results

  • Total energy used before solar: 8130 kWh
  • Peak demand: 34.90 kW
  • Grid import after solar: 2549 kWh
  • Solar self-consumption ratio: 81.29%
  • Estimated monthly solar benefit: $1626.99
  • Simple payback: 1.84 years

Assumptions

The load data is simulated for a small commercial building. Solar generation is modelled using a simplified daytime generation curve. Tariffs, emissions factor and solar system cost are assumed for concept-level analysis.
